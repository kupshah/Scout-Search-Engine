      The Galactic Arena at Selected Frasers Centrepoint Malls

      Battle it out at the Galactic Arena in our 9 malls where you’ll be faced with exciting missions like getting a family makeover to dress as your favourite STAR WARSTM character, flying a Millennium Falcon drone and plenty more fun from 17 November to 24 December 2017!

      Step inside the Galactic Arena with no spending tagged and embark on an adventure in a galaxy far, far away.  

      Affirm your light side or dark side allegiance and dress the part in a STAR WARSTM Family Makeover, immerse yourself in a VR experience, learn to fly a Millennium Falcon drone, find the right path for BB-8 in a maze, and finish off your adventure with some retail therapy.
