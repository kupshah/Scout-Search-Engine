Here Are All of Ron Howard’s Twitter and Instagram Updates on Solo: A Star Wars Story

Check out every photo the acclaimed director has released during production.

StarWars.com Team

The untitled Han Solo film is currently in production, and director Ron Howard has been documenting his experience on Twitter and Instagram — with everything from photos with cast members to teases of vehicle controls to shots of his morning coffee. And we love it all. So if you want to revisit some of his updates, if you’ve never seen them, or if you might have missed some, fear not: you can find them all here, and we’ll update every time a new photo is released.
