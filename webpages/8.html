Constance Clarke is a fictional character played by Miranda Raison in a series of audio plays produced by Big Finish Productions based on the long-running British science fiction television series Doctor Who. She is a companion of the Sixth Doctor.

Character history[edit]

The character first appears in The End of the Line, one of the stories in the audio anthology The Sixth Doctor: The Last Adventure. Her first chronological appearance and meeting with the Doctor came in Criss-Cross, where she is introduced as a leading WREN of the Women's Royal Naval Service during World War II. In 1944, she meets the Sixth Doctor while working at Bletchley Park and forces him to take her with him on his travels, wanting to be taken back to her duties once she decides to finish travelling. After several trips, in Quicksilver the two are joined by Flip Jackson, a former companion of the Doctor, for further adventures.
