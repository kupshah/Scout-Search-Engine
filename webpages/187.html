Get Into the Holiday Spirit Because Freeform’s 25 Days of Christmas Schedule Is Here

Crystal Nguyen

Oh My Disney Contributor

If radio stations can start playing holiday music in November, then it’s definitely not too early for us to start planning for one of our favorite times of the year! We don’t know about you, but pretty much every night of December is booked for us now that Freeform’s 25 Days of Christmas schedule is out. Catch us sipping on hot cocoa as we celebrate the season with our holiday favorites, including iconic films like Frozen, The Santa Clause, and Disney’s A Christmas Carol.
