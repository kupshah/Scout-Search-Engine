"Parish Church"?[edit]

The article says that the Pauline Chapel is the Parish Church for the Vatican. I don't think this is accurate. The Parish Church is the Church of Saint Anne.J.J. Bustamante 19:15, 28 June 2007 (UTC)

I removed the erroneous assertion. See, for example, "as I visit St Anne's parish, the parish of Vatican City. This is, in fact, my parish." (Homily of John Paul II given at Sant'Anna dei Palafrenieri, 10-Dec-1978). Wonderbreadsf (talk) 20:23, 18 April 2010 (UTC)
