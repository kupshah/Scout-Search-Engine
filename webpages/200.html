Mark Hamill Surprised Guests on Star Tours and Their Faces Are Priceless

Crystal Nguyen

Oh My Disney Contributor

We know that exploring a galaxy far, far away comes with many surprises, but what are the chances of running into Mark Hamill himself? It sounds too good to be true, but for a group of lucky passengers on a recent Star Tours flight at Disneyland it actually happened, and the once-in-a-lifetime moment was caught on camera on the Official Star Wars Instagram! Check it out:

The Force is strong with @hamillhimself as he surprised guests on #StarTours @Disneyland. #TheLastJedi #StarWars

A post shared by Star Wars (@starwars) on Nov 14, 2017 at 7:10pm PST

Here’s another look:

It’s Luke Skywalker!!!!! All of the guests’ range of reactions are basically us right now watching this video—from the people immediately whipping out their phones to the guy in the front putting on his glasses in disbelief. The Force is so strong with this moment—we would have probably burst into tears of joy on the spot. This truly made us so happy. It’s a good day to be a Star Wars fan. (Like every day!)
