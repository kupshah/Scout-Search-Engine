Welcome to WikiProject European Microstates a collaboration to improve articles related to Andorra, Liechtenstein, Malta, Monaco, San Marino and the Vatican City.

Scope[edit]

This WikiProject is devoted to writing new articles and enhancing existing coverage of the European Microstates:

  • Andorra
  • Liechtenstein
  • Malta
  • Monaco
  • San Marino
  • Vatican City
  • Gibraltar (partly separate state, British Overseas Territory)

The work will not be limited in scope, and will cover government, international relations, culture, sport, and all other aspects of society. The project is branched into work groups and subprojects.
