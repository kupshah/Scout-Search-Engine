The 1977 Romika Cup was a men's tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts at the MTTC Iphitos in Munich, West Germany. It was part of the 1977 Grand Prix tennis circuit and categorized as a Two Star event. The tournament was held from 26 April through 1 May 1977. Željko Franulović won the singles title.[3]

Winners[edit]

Men's Singles[edit]

Main article: 1977 Romika Cup – Singles

Željko Franulović defeated Víctor Pecci Sr. 6–1, 6–1, 6–7, 7–5

  • It was Franulović's only title of the year and the 13th of his career.

Men's Doubles[edit]

Main article: 1977 Romika Cup – Doubles

František Pala / Balázs Taróczy defeated Nikola Špear / John Whitlinger 6–3, 6–4

  • It was Pala's only title of the year and the 1st of his career. It was Taróczy's only title of the year and the 3rd of his career.
