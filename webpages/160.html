Blessed Aaron of Auxerre
Died ca. 807
Auxerre, France
Venerated in Roman Catholic Church
(Archdiocese of Sens)
Feast 28 September

Aaron of Auxerre (fl. ~ 800) was a bishop of Auxerre. He has been beatified by the Roman Catholic Church, and his relics are venerated in the Church of Saint-Germain in Auxerre, where his feast day is celebrated on 28 September.

He is included in the roster of saints in the book Les Petits Bollandistes.

References[edit]

  • Holweck, F. G., A Biographical Dictionary of the Saints. St. Louis, Mo.: B. Herder Book Co. 1924.
