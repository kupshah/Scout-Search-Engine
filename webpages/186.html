Why Stay at a Hotel at the Disneyland Resort?

Enjoy Disneyland Resort hotel benefits designed to help you experience a truly magical vacation. Benefits include:

  • Extra Magic Hour early admission available every day of your hotel stay, to select attractions, stores, entertainment and dining locations at either Disneyland Park or Disney California Adventure Park one hour before the park opens to the general public (valid theme park admission and active hotel room key required).
  • Close proximity to Disneyland enchantment, putting the spectacular sights and experiences of the Disneyland theme parks just steps away!
  • Attentive service, legendary Disney quality, luxurious amenities, including the Mandara Spa at Disney's Grand Californian Hotel & Spa—and an array of dining choices.

Important Information About the Extra Magic Hour Benefit
The current Extra Magic Hour schedule allows registered Resort hotel Guests access to one park on select days: Disneyland Park on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays, and Disney California Adventure Park on Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays and Sundays.

For admission, each registered hotel Guest (ages 3 and older) must have a valid theme park ticket and an active hotel room key card. To take full advantage of the Extra Magic Hour experience, we strongly recommended that you arrive at least one hour and 15 minutes prior to regular park opening.

Applicable theme park days and times of operation and all other elements including, but not limited to, operation of attractions, entertainment, stores and restaurants and appearances of Characters may vary and are subject to change without notice. Behold a Magical Makeover

A stay in the newly renovated Disney’s Grand Californian Hotel & Spa will reveal just how much our most upscale retreat is truly far beyond compare.

Exceptional service, fine dining at Napa Rose and one-of-a-kind views of Disney California Adventure Park have always drawn Guests to this stunning accommodation, but now there’s a new reason to visit—completely redesigned rooms.

The refreshed spaces feature an array of family-friendly amenities, upgraded bathrooms and California-inspired artwork created by Disney Imagineers.

And that’s not all! The Great Hall lobby, pool areas and concierge lounge have also received a host of welcoming enhancements.

Fun Reasons to Visit the Disneyland Resort

Experience countless magical moments at the Disneyland Resort when you add theme park admission. You’ll discover wonder, joy and excitement around every turn when you visit our 2 amazing theme parks and the bustling Downtown Disney District.

Disneyland Park is where it all began. With its mix of new and classic attractions and entertainment, Walt’s original theme park is home to timeless fun. Here, you can:

  • Meet and snap photos with beloved Characters like Mickey, Tinker Bell and some of your favorite Disney Princesses.
  • Set sail on a swashbuckling voyage with the Pirates of the Caribbean.
  • Plummet down a 5-story waterfall on Splash Mountain.
  • Embark on an epic Star Wars-themed expedition on Star Tours—The Adventures Continue and check out Star Wars Launch Bay, an out-of-this-world exhibit.
  • Wind down your day by taking in dazzling fireworks on select nights.

For more escapades inspired by Disney and Pixar tales, head over to Disney California Adventure Park, where you can:

  • Embark on a thrilling, free-fall adventure on the all-new Guardians of the Galaxy – Mission: BREAKOUT! attraction.
  • Take flight on Soarin’ Around the World, a newly reimagined journey above some of the most celebrated natural wonders and landmarks.
  • Gear up for high-octane action in Cars Land with zippy attractions such as Radiator Springs Racers and Mater’s Junkyard Jamboree.
  • View riveting entertainment like Frozen – Live at the Hyperion and the awesome, after-dark spectacle World of Color.

Attractions and entertainment may be seasonal and are subject to change without notice. Please check show guide for availability and times.
