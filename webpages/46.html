A disruption is ongoing but again not that serious - the trains running 20 minutes late. A blackout is sudden and catastrophic - a whole town losing electricity. So defects and blackouts are both abrupt failures, probably only one is notable, whereas a disruption won't generally be a failure (the trains not running at all).Le Deluge (talk) 00:38, 23 December 2016 (UTC)
