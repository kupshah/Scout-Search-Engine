The 2005 Movistar Open was an ATP men's tennis tournament held in Viña del Mar, Chile. It was the 12th edition of the tournament and was held from January 31 to February 6. Gastón Gaudio won the singles title.

Winners[edit]

Men's Singles[edit]

Main article: 2005 Movistar Open – Singles

Gastón Gaudio defeated Fernando González 6–3, 6–4

  • It was Gaudio's 1st title of the year and the 6th of his career.


Men's Doubles[edit]

Main article: 2005 Movistar Open – Doubles

David Ferrer / Santiago Ventura defeated Gastón Etlis / Martín Rodríguez 6–3, 6–4

  • It was Ferrer's 1st title of the year and the 2nd of his career. It was Ventura's 1st title of the year and the 2nd of his career.
