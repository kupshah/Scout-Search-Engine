The Last Jedi // OCTOBER 12, 2017

Here Are All of Rian Johnson’s Twitter and Instagram Updates on Star Wars: The Last Jedi

Check out the director's personal behind-the-scenes looks at the next film in the Star Wars saga.

StarWars.com Team

Star Wars: The Last Jedi arrives on December 15, 2017 — tickets are on sale now — and director Rian Johnson has been offering fans glimpses behind the scenes via his personal Twitter and Instagram accounts. StarWars.com has compiled all of Johnson’s updates into this handy post, which we’ll keep current with each new release. May the raw, untamed power of these tweets, photos, and videos be with you.
