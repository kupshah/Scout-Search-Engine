The Force Awakens at Lazada.sg

From the most popular droid of the moment, BB-8 by Sphero, or the amazingly detailed Monopoly set, and Black Series figurines by Hasbro, the latest products from Star Wars: The Force Awakens have ignited our imaginations and brought home the latest chapter of Star Wars.

There's a Star Wars product for everyone, and the best place to explore the full range is at Lazada.sg, both online and mobile.

Featuring the incredibly customisable Star Wars Bladebuilders: Jedi Master Lightsaber by Hasbro, the classic range of Lego Buildable Watches, and of course the huge selection of Disney Infinity 3.0 Figures - it's time to make some room on the shelf!

With easy access to these products wherever you are. All that awaits is for you to make the first click to Lazada.sg! Impressive. Most impressive.
